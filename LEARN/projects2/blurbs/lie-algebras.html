Projects and Assignments in Pure Mathematics

Lie algebras are key objects in many areas of modern mathematics and theoretical physics. The aim of a 3MM project would be basic structure and classification theorem (including root systems); roughly corresponding to the first 4 chapters of Humphrey's book. For a 4MM project some representation theory would be included.

Prerequisites: A thorough understanding of level 2 linear algebra.

Books

Erdmann, Karin & Wildon, Mark: "Introduction to Lie Algebras", Springer, 2006.
Humphreys, James E.: "Introduction to Lie Algebras and Representation Theory", Springer 1978.
Fulton, William & Harris Joe: "Representation Theory, A First Course", Springer 1991
and many others
