Morse Theory

Morse theory concerns the study of critical points of a differentiable function. These generalise the maxima, minima and saddle points of a real valued function of 2 variables. In the more general case the domain of the function is a manifold, which is a higher dimensional version of a surface.

Prerequisites: MATH2051 Geometry of Curves and Surfaces

Books

V. Guillemin and A. Pollack, Differential Topology.
J.W. Milnor, Morse Theory.
