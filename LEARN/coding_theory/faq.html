MATH3153/5153 FAQ 2017

Q. Can I give in exercise attempts for feedback?
A. Yes - any time.
Q. What will come up in tutorials?
A. Anything you like from the course. Just let us know what you need help with.
Q. Where are the solutions for sheet X (insert X as appropriate)?
A. They are made available a few days after the questions become relevant.
Q. What is the difference between 3153 and 5153 (apart from the scheduling)?
A. For a quick answer, compare the syllabi. For more details now, have a chat with the lecturer. For even more details, please wait until week 5.
Q. The lectures are too hard (/too easy), what can I do?
A. Obviously if some people find lectures too hard and some find them too easy (as often happens at the beginning of a module)
then we cannot address this by simply making them harder or easier! If you said "too easy" then please be patient - they will get harder.
If you said "too hard", then please let the lecturer or tutor know exactly something that you found hard, and we will help via tutorial support.
