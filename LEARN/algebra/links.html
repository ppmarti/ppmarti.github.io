Basic Algebra LINKS and REFERENCES

The following sets of notes may provide useful support for your studies.
  • May's list for finite topological spaces
  • Barmak's thesis on finite topological spaces
  • Stanley N. Burris and H.P. Sankappanavar --- A Course in Universal Algebra

Some useful textbooks

  • (Beginning) Finite Mathematics (Schaum Outline Series), Seymour Lipschutz.
    (Sadly the old version of this book is out of print, and the new version is not due to be ready until April 2004! In the mean time we will have to go back to recommending two (fortunately both very inexpensive) books - see below.)
  • Discrete Mathematics (Schaum Outline Series), Seymour Lipschutz.
    (Covers Set Theory, Matrices, Recurrence Relations)
  • Mathematical Methods for Science Students (Longman), G Stephenson.
    (Covers Complex Numbers, Matrices, Series, Difference Equations)
