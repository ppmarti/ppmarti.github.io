Topology

This is a learning resource page for Topology, for undergraduates.
It is independent of any particular module or programme.
The material here may thus be of use to any 3rd year Mathematics (or other) student studying Topology (but do check your syllabus). See for example the Leeds Uni module MATH3225 HERE .

Some useful textbooks

  • E Moise, Geometric topology in dimensions 2 and 3 (Springer)
  • M A Armstrong, Basic Topology (McGraw-Hill)
  • B Mendelson, Introduction to Topology (Blackie)
  • G Stephenson, Mathematical Methods for Science Students, Longman Publishers. (Contains numerous good exercises.)
  • M R Spiegel, Advanced Calculus (Schaum), McGraw Hill Publishers.
